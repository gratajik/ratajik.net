Ratajik Software - Information

Ratajik Software was formed in 1992, providing various software for the OS/2 platform, including utilities, multimedia applications, internet applications, and games.  Over twenty titles were released over a four year period, with users from primarily the United States and Europe, with a mix of Asian customers.  

After the general collapse of the OS/2 market in the mid-1990's, a shift of focus was made to the Windows platform.  To date, nine titles have been released, focusing primarily on utilities for the Internet.

Ratajik Software is a LLC, based out of Columbia, South Carolina, USA. To contact us, please e-mail ratajiksoftware@ratajik.com
